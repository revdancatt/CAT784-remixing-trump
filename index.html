About

The text above is generated using a simple Markov Chain engine using the transcript of Trump's Press Conference of the 16th Feb 2017. The questions have been removed and some punctuation cleaned up so just Trump's words are used.

There's some background about Markov Chains and what's going on here: Notes on remixing with generative text and Markov chains.
